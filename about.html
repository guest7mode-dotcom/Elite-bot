About Nurturing High

A Discord‑based academy inspired by psychological anime and survival schools.

The concept

Students enter Nurturing High with nothing guaranteed. Effort, strategy, and social skills decide who climbs the class ranks, controls the points, and survives each semester.

Economy & classes

Every choice affects your personal balance and your homeroom’s future. Classes compete for points, status, and special advantages granted by the Headmaster and student council.

Clubs & cards

Clubs create mini‑communities with their own leaders, events, and rewards. Collectible cards represent powerful students and icons from different series, unlocked through the in‑server economy and events.

How to join

Join the Discord, register with the bot, read the rules carefully, and pick a club. From there, your story at Nurturing High is completely up to you.
